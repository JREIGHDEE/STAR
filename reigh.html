Part 2: From Reigh

To my Beloved,

Now, here I am in college, and I can’t believe how far I’ve come. Or maybe, how far I had to go to understand who I am. I wasn’t just a girl. I’m a boy. I’ve always been a boy, even if I didn’t realize it then. But in this space, away from the confusion and the fears, I finally saw it. I finally saw me. And it felt like everything fell into place. I remember thinking, What if she doesn’t see me the same? What if I lose her forever?

But then, I realized something else. My love for her hadn’t changed. If anything, it had only grown stronger, deeper. It had always been there, even when I was pretending not to feel it. Even when I was too scared to accept it. I’m Reigh now. But my heart… my heart is the same.

I think she knew. She must have. She saw me, in every form – the girl, the boy, all the versions of me that have existed. But no matter who I was, she always loved me the same.

I remember one day, sitting next to her, and feeling this wave of truth come over me. It was like I wanted to say, Do you know? Do you see me for who I really am? And even though I didn’t speak it aloud, I could feel that she did. And she was still here. She was still holding onto me, still steady through it all. I wasn’t just a confused girl anymore, and yet, she was still there. That meant everything to me.

I remember thinking, I’ve changed. But has my love for her changed? The answer was simple – no. It had never wavered.

And now, here we are, together. So different from before, yet everything still feels the same.

I am who I am. She is who she is. But our love, it’s always been the same.
Always her.
Always me.
Always us.
